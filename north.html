North indian

  1. Rogan Josh

    2. The red color of rogan josh comes from the Kashmiri dry red chilies used to prepare it. There are many versions of this dish. Here is one you should enjoy.

  2. Fish Amritsari

    3. This fried fish dish is named after the city where it originated. Use firm-fleshed white fish to make this great appetizer or side dish to complement the main course.

  3. Palak Paneer

    4. This mildly flavored dish is super healthy too! If paneer (farmer's cheese or dry curd cheese) is not on hand or you want a dairy-free option, you also can use tofu or veggies like cauliflower or potato.

  4. Rajma Dal

    5. Serve this red kidney bean curry or rajma dal with plain boiled rice, kachumber salad, and your favorite pickle.

  5. Jalebi Recipe

    6. This crispy, syrupy dessert is well-loved everywhere in India. It can be compared to that perennial favorite fair food served in the States—funnel cakes—except at the end, these fried treats are soaked in a sugary syrup.
